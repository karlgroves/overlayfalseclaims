Appendix D: Listed customers vs. reality for Allyable

While researching the overlays, I discovered that Allyable.com lists 15 logos under the heading "Trusted By", implying those were customers. Of those 15, only 5 could be considered customers. However, of that 5, 1 is a reseller and 3 are customers of "make-sense.com" which apparently uses Allyable code.Â  Ultimately, 73% of the claimed/ implied customers do not use Allyable on their websites at all and only 1 of the listed companies is a direct customer of Allyable.
